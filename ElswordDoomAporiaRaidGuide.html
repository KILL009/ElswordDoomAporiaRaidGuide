1-1:

Upon Raid Entry:

Party splits up to direct an electricity beam into all of the nodes, then the final destination.

The node is directed by players standing in front of it, if you face the direction the beam is coming from, the beam will go down.

The connectors are always on the same floor and will look something like this

THIS MECH HAPPENS 3 TIMES AND THE CONNECTORS CHANGE POSITIONS EACH TIME

You have 1 minute to complete the mech, the timer resets each time the lightning reaches the end. Failure means a raid wipe

Extra strategy: Do not complete the mechanic right away. Stall for green set to be off cooldown at the beginning of the fight.

Type-E Echidna:

200 HP Bars | 12T HP | 83.9% Defense | DR: 60%

(For reference on Damage Reduction: Serpentium & Challenge Modes all have 80%)

Forced Mechanic at 160x/120x/80x/40x

GUARDLEVEL Gauge:

Whenever a party member is hit by a normal pattern, the gauge increases.

Dodge attacks when possible.

Whenever GUARDLEVEL goes up 1 stage, he forces Mechanic 1.

GUARDLEVEL stages increase the amount of lasers from the attacks

GUARDLEVEL gauge has a number of attacks associated with it, but to be honest I’ve never had anyone even get hit by any of them so it does not seem useful to call out.

Stand close(almost running inside) the boss to avoid most patterns. 

Important Pattern: Target Laser

1 Player will get targeted from above with lines. Bring them away from the party, as the lasers are not iframeable. They lock position to target after 2 seconds.

I’ll add the important boss attacks here because I do think now that it is important to dodge.

Adding like in a day or two

The boss periodically moves between set locations. The locations he can move to are marked.

 Green Strategy: Use on normal patterns after he has just moved, or during groggy periods depending on party speed.

Freeze Strategy: Boss has 2 freeze count, 30s CD, use off CD.

Boss does a Forced Mechanic at 160x/120x/80x/40x

Forced Mechanic comes from a pool of 3, they can repeat multiple times.

Mechanic 1: Hyper Charge

Under the 6 set locations, boss will spawn a shape. 5 of these locations will have the same shape, and one will be the other shape

(5 green squares and 1 blue diamond, or

 5 blue diamonds and 1 green square)


The boss will appear at the location with a different shape and eat it. He will also eat any players nearby/on the shape, making them essentially stunned until the mech is over.

After he appears, 3 players must use Hyperactive to stop the charging.

Afterwards, the boss enters groggy.

 Groggy Duration: ~9s

If 3 players do not use Hyperactive near the boss, raid wipe

Note: People must be assigned into two groups to Hyperactive, as this pattern might get spammed a lot and if a 4th person uses it, there may not be enough for the next time he does it.

 Recommended

People are now usually waiting to use the 3rd Hyperactive to use the charging time as a DPS window, you have ~5-6 seconds before the raid wipes to use it.

Mechanic 2: Gas

 If you stand in the gas and take lots of damage with this debuff, it is fake

 If you stand in the gas and take low damage with this debuff, it is real.

[엘소드/Elsword KR] 데이브레이커 예언의 초석 [ 3단계 ] 클리어 22:30 / DayBreaker 21-1 [ Lv.3 ] Clear 22:30

Boss will release gas under the 6 set locations. Players must split up to find the real gas pile, and all stand in the real gas to prevent wipe attack. Repeat this a total of 3 times. At the end of the third time, boss enters groggy.

 Groggy Duration: ~9s

Players not in the safe gas will get hit by lethal attack.  

Mechanic 3: Grab, Target, Laser

These are the gates on the floor. There are 5 and you can see them on the map shown at the top of the section.

Ref:[엘소드/Elsword KR] 데이브레이커 예언의 초석 [ 3단계 ] 클리어 22:30 / DayBreaker 21-1 [ Lv.3 ] Clear 22:30

Players must split up and each stand on one of the 5 ground gates. (6th player should just stand in between a gate)


The boss picks a gate to come out of, it grabs the player on it and slowly does damage to them.

Teammates must free the player by throwing a consumable (Orb/Spear). 

The released player will have a target mark above their head. The boss will repeat this 3 times.

If two players are grabbed at the same time(aka two players are on the same ground gate), the boss does a raid wipe.

Players who were grabbed may be marked with a green square, blue diamond or red circle target. A laser will be aimed at them. Marked players must go to the shape that matches their mark to aim the laser to destroy the shape.

Track the debuffs of the grabbed players. If you have a green square and blue diamond already, then all players can get off the grate so no one is given the red circle.

If there is a red circle, that gets a death laser aimed at them. Move it away from the party then the marked player needs to get away from it after the laser's direction is locked. The red circle player must also get away from it.

Failure to break both shape with the respective lasers is raid wipe

 Groggy Duration: ~9s

Additional Commentary:

A lot of patterns are close safe, but you need to be really close. There is an In then Out safe pattern that increases meter a lot if everyone is close.

 It is possible just to wipe from increasing guard level too much, and having Mech 1 forced when no one has Hypers, even when they are grouped properly.

1-2: Barkat

250 HP Bars | 12T HP | 83.9% Defense | DR: 80%

Forced Mechanic at 200x/150x/100x/50x/25x

Freeze Patterns: Boss has ice attacks that give you freeze stacks, each stack lowers your move speed.

If you hit 10 stacks, you will get frozen/caged. Easily broken by ally commands. (Just a few hits, actives don’t work)

All stacks are removed if you time out the 30s

Green Strategy: Groggy after forced patterns, or use whenever Medusa is not coming up

Freeze Strategy: Boss has 1 freeze count, 90s CD, use off CD.

(Important) Normal Pattern: Medusa

Failed Medusa with follow up

On a 30 second cooldown, she will do a Medusa pattern.

All players are given an eye and within 1 second, they are expected to be looking at or away from the boss for around 1-2 seconds.

(The boss also shows an eye that is always open, only pay attention to the one above your head)

If the eye is open, players must look TOWARDS the boss. If the eye is closed, players must look AWAY.

If any player fails, the boss will hit the whole map which stuns players who failed, then spawns a shadow hand under every player.

Players hit by shadow hand attack will receive lethal damage and a Shadow Debuff.


Note: Players should be grouped in case someone fails. This allows there to be safe areas to dodge the shadow hands.

The stun can be cleansed

The Shadow Debuff stacks up to 2 times and decreases defense per stack. On the 3rd stack, it kills you through everything but Res title. (AKA kills through SoT, so res title immediately if you are going to hit 3 stacks)

IMPORTANT: Under 26 bars, players can have different medusa eyes(open or closed).

Boss alternates 2 Mechanics in either order at 200x/150x/100x/50x

Mechanic 1: Double Laser

Check for this buff to see which you have to dodge

The boss teleports to the background and covers the sides in a black fog. In the middle, Ice Lasers shoot down from above

Shadow Hands raise from below

Move to the center and check your buff bar:
Red buff means you are immune to the shadows, so stand in the shadows and dodge the ice.

Purple buff means you are immune to the ice, so stand in the ice and dodge the shadows

(How I remember it is the purple buff has the ghost icon, so I should dodge the ghostly shadow hands) 

The buff is random every wave and might  change. The whole party should have the same buff.

Getting hit by shadow hands will still give you Shadow Debuff stack, and ice lasers will still give you ice stacks. (You can get frozen in this mech)

There is no Res Null during this mech.

Careful when you proc SoT or Res Title, as you can get KD’d. Make sure you don’t roll off to the sides, as being in the black fog does lots of damage.


After the mech, the boss enters groggy

Groggy Duration: ~7s

Mechanic 2: Red Light, Green Light

Players are transformed into little demons. Move to the boss but:

Move if the eyes are closed.

Stop if the eyes are about to open.


If you are caught, you will be unable to move until the end of the mechanic.

Hit the boss a few times with your demon Z command to complete the mech. Even one person making it to the end is enough.

Raid wipe if the boss does not take enough command damage or immediately if everyone gets caught.


Clear the mech to get a groggy window

Groggy Duration: ~7s

You can use >> to dash, but it will make you slide more so you have to stop earlier.

At 25x, boss does final mechanic

Mechanic 3: Final Phase

Cutscene plays, upon entry a berserk timer is added at 1:29 and starts to count down

A module is placed at the center of the map, 4 players must stick to it and repeatedly click F1 to charge the meter above it (F1 has 1.5s ICD). Once it is full, it adds 49 seconds to the berserk time.

Pattern is repeated every 60 seconds from when the pattern first starts.

The timer running out is a raid wipe. You do not have infinite time and you will run out of time within ~8-10 times of this mechanic happening.

Filling the meter makes the boss teleport to the middle and enter groggy.

Groggy Duration: ~7s

At the same time, the boss starts at the left in a grey zone. The other two players should stand close and watch the boss. (Host should be one)

The boss is holding a flame.  Use F1 the moment the flame disappears to groggy her. This is a dps window but the rest of the party should continue charging the middle module.

She glows red if you use it too early, pushing and damaging the whole party, while delaying the flame removal timing.

After 5s of groggy, the boss gets back up and teleports to the other side. This repeats until the module meter is filled

If two players do not hit F1 on the boss, or it is done too late, it destroys the module, meaning you will not be able to add time to the timer, causing raid wipe for low damage teams.

If your timer is close to over and you have no more time to fill the module, the boss takes full damage and debuff during this attack, so you can fully send damage to try to kill.

Newly Added Pattern: Triple Medusa

Shares cooldown with the main mechanic, usually done after groggy/failure of the module charging

Shows medusa eyes over pairs of players three times. Put on Res title during this.

Shadow hands start to be fired from the boss, at each pair in the order that they got the eyes. Dodge them as they give the Shadow Debuff.

You can watch your own debuff bar. When the 3s timer runs out, the shadow hands have their target locked so use that timing to dodge.

The Shadow Debuff stacks up to 2 times and decreases defense per stack. On the 3rd stack, it kills you through everything but Res title.

Additional Commentary:

Her ice patterns are not mentioned. They seem self explanatory with a video watch or just doing the dungeon.

The boss’s hitbox expands when she does patterns that spread her wings. This makes them decent patterns to greed but you need to watch your freeze stacks.

The last section is effectively a DPS check of 1.2T HP, but realistically you are not time failing if you can do the mech properly. The issue arises when people are constantly dying to Triple Medusa, as being dead means you are doing no damage.
